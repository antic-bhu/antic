ABOUT THE EVENT

We are happy to announce that the Vishlesan Innovative Ideas contest (VIIC-2022) in conjunction with ANTIC-2022 conference is going to be organized by the Department of Computer Science, Banaras Hindu University, Varanasi in collaboration with Technical Innovation Hub, Indian Institute of Technology Patna (TIH, IIT Patna).
IIT Patna Vishlesan I-Hub Foundation with domains Speech, Video and Text Analytics is a section 8 company founded at IIT Patna, which aims to create a strong and seamles ecosystem for leveraging the potential and exponential growth of interdisciplinary cyber physical systems (ICPSs) under Department of Science & Technology (DST).

There is no participation fee for this event.

WHERE

Due to COVID-19, the Conference will be hosted in Online Mode.
Department of Computer Science, Banaras Hindu University, India

WHEN

Thursday-Saturday
22-24 December, 2022

Objectives

This contest is to give an opportunity to highly innovative ideas for the start-up at TIH, IIT Patna with funding up to 30 Lakh.

Eligibility

Teams of up to 4 members (Indian citizens only) are eligible. Multidisciplinary ideas are strongly encouraged to apply.

Highlights of the Contest

Call for innovative ideas are open in the areas of Speech, Text and Video analysis, AI & IoT, Drone &counter drone technologies, Point Care Diagnostics, Embedded systems, Nanotechnologies, Civil & Defense Cyber Security, RF & Telematics.
The best three ideas would be awarded with rewards.
One best idea can be further supported financially for the start-up up to 30 Lakh.

Evaluation Criteria

Each project will be executed by three reviewers.
The evaluation will be mainly based on the following key points: innovation, societal contribution, technology adopted, cost incurred and its commercial implications.

Prize

Top three trailblazing ideas which aim at bringing in a revolution shall be awarded with attractive prizes.

1st Prize

Rs. 40,000/-

2nd Prize

Rs. 30,000/-

3rd Prize

Rs. 20,000/-


Submission

Every innovative idea will be submitted in the form of presentation as per the prescribed format with voice notes (if required), by using following link

Idea Submission

Only selected ideas will be called for the presentation and demonstration.

Presentation Format

Expected Outcome

The evaluation will be mainly based on the following key points: innovation, societal contribution, technology adopted, cost incurred and its commercial implications.
Shortlisted teams will be invited to present their solution during the conference. They are also welcomed to demonstrate the working prototype.

IMPORTANT DATES

Last Date for Submission

November 07, 2022

Last Date of Acceptance Notification

November 30, 2022

Final Presentation

December 22-24, 2022
