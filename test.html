Dear Prof./Researcher,

Greetings from Department of Information Technology Atal Bihari Vajpayee Indian Institute of Information Technology and Management, Gwalior, India.

We are glad to inform you that the Department of Information Technology Atal Bihari Vajpayee Indian Institute of Information Technology and Management, Gwalior, India is organizing 5th International Conference on Advanced Network Technologies and Intelligent Computing (ANTIC-2025) in offline mode (Online presentations will be permitted exclusively for foreign authors and a limited number of Indian authors who submit a genuine request) from 21st to 23rd December 2025. The proceedings of conference will be submitted for publication in Communications in Computer and Information Science (CCIS) series of Springer [SCOPUS Indexed].

You are cordially invited to submit high quality research papers in the areas of Advanced Network Technologies and Intelligent Computing. The topics covered (but are not limited to) in the conference are as follows:
