Call for Workshop Proposals

5th International Conference on Advanced Network Technologies and Intelligent Computing

December 21‚Äì23, 2025 | ABV-IIITM Gwalior, India

The Department of Information Technology at Atal Bihari Vajpayee ‚Äì Indian Institute of Information Technology and Management (ABV-IIITM), Gwalior, India, is pleased to announce the 5th International Conference on Advanced Network Technologies and Intelligent Computing (ANTIC-2025), to be held inoffline mode from December 21 to 23, 2025.

In addition to the main conference program, ANTIC-2025 will feature a series of workshops aimed at showcasing cutting-edge research on focused topics of high interest. These workshops are intended to foster dialogue, encourage collaboration, and provide a platform for presenting early-stage research in a more interactive and informal setting than the main conference.

We invite workshop proposals from individuals or teams interested in organizing engaging sessions on topics related to the conference themes. Preference will be given to workshops focused on emerging trends in Advanced Network Technologies and Intelligent Computing.

üìù Proposal Guidelines

Workshop proposals should include the following information:

  • Title, scope, and relevant topics of the workshop
  • Names, affiliations, and short bios of the organizers
  • Tentative organizing/steering committee, if applicable
  • Workshop format (e.g., paper presentations, demos, panels, etc.)
    • Indicate whether a half-day or full-day workshop is preferred
    • The workshop length may be changed later depending on the number of paper submissions received
  • Past history of the workshop (if previously held), including:
    • Number of submissions
    • Accepted papers
    • Attendees
  • Rationale for the workshop
    • Why is the topic timely and significant?
    • Is it an emerging area?
    • Why would it attract high-quality submissions and participants?
  • Dissemination plan, including strategies for publicity and online presence
  • Draft Call for Papers (CFP)

Note: At least one organizer of each accepted workshop must be physically present during the event.
