ABOUT WORKSHOP

The Department of Information Technology at ABV-IIITM, Gwalior proudly presents the 1st International Workshop on Advancing AI and ML Across Disciplines: Use Cases and Emerging Applications (AAMLAD-2025), to be held from December 21‚Äì23, 2025 in Gwalior, India in offline mode (with limited online participation). The accepted and presented papers of this workshop will be published as open-access proceedings in the CCIS series of Springer (Scopus-indexed).

This event offers a vibrant platform for researchers, scientists, and students to present groundbreaking work, engage in technical sessions, and explore the AI and ML Applications across disciplines.

Key Benefits

  • üìÑ Open-access publication opportunities in CCIS series of Springer (SCOPUS Indexed).
  • üåè Global audience & expert networking.
  • üí° Workshops & panel discussions with industry leaders.

We invite the submission of original research papers to this workshop. This workshop seeks to unite pioneering research from academia and industry, highlighting transformative use cases and emerging methodologies in AI and ML. We invite contributions spanning a wide array of AI and ML domains, including but not limited to deep learning, natural language processing, computer vision, reinforcement learning, generative modeling, explainable AI, federated learning, edge AI, cloud/edge computing, quantum computing, and ethical AI. We particularly encourage submissions that demonstrate cross-disciplinary applications in fields such as healthcare, finance, education, agriculture, cybersecurity, robotics, environmental sciences, and the social sciences. By bringing together diverse perspectives, the workshop aims to promote knowledge exchange, foster collaboration, and drive innovation at the intersection of AI, ML, and real-world applications.

SCOPES and TOPICS

  • Advanced deep learning models and optimization strategies
  • Natural language understanding and conversational AI
  • Computer vision, image, and video analysis
  • Reinforcement learning with single and multi-agent systems
  • Generative AI and synthetic data creation
  • Explainable and transparent machine learning
  • Federated and distributed AI frameworks
  • Cloud/Edge computing for AI-enabled devices
  • Responsible and ethical AI practices
  • AI for healthcare diagnostics and medical research
  • Fintech innovations and intelligent trading systems
  • Smart education and adaptive learning platforms
  • AI-powered agriculture and sustainable farming
  • Cybersecurity analytics and automated threat detection
  • Robotics, automation, and autonomous navigation
  • Environmental monitoring and climate prediction
  • Social science research and computational humanities
  • Urban planning, smart cities, and infrastructure optimization
